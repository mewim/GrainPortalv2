User Guide

Login/Sign Up

  • User will be directed to the login page once the url is entered.
  • Fill up the “username”, and “password” field, then click “login” button to login
  • Click the “Sign Up” button to create a new user
  • Click Request New Password

Dashboard

  • User will be directed to Dashboard page once logged in.
  • It is the “homepage” of the portal which provides an overview of user’s information, including numbers of notifications, reports, or sensor information.
  • It also provides visualization of historical information from sensors.
  • Click on any of the colored blocks to access corresponding functionalities.
  • Farmers, traders, and researchers will have different information on dashboard

Sidebar

  • It will appear at the same position on most page.
  • User can easily access all the functions of the portal by clicking the items on the sidebar.
  • It is expandable and beautiful

I. Farmer Portal

Notification

  • Click the Notification Icon on the sidebar to access this function.
  • It has two parts – Inbox and Archived.
  • Inbox are notifications for farmers to read.
  • Users can archive a notification once after reading it.

Sensors

  • Click the Sensors Icon on the sidebar to access this function.
  • It has two parts – Management and Readings.
  • Management provides a way of adding and deleting sensors.
  • Readings displays historical records of a specific sensor.

Reports

  • Click the Reports Icon on the sidebar to access this function.
  • It has two parts – Create and View.
  • Create page is used to create new report.
  • View page is used to view created reports.

Market

  • Click the Market Icon on the sidebar to access this function.
  • It has three parts – List, Selling and Sold.
  • List shows all available reports user can post on the market.
  • Click “List” Button in List Section to post a report.
  • Selling shows all posted reports of the user on the market.
  • Click “Remove” Button in Selling Section to remove a report from the market.

Q&A

  • Click the Q&A Icon on the sidebar to access this function.
  • It has three parts – Ask, Answered, Unanswered.
  • Ask page is used to post a new question.
  • Answered page displays all answered questions.
  • Unanswered page displays all unanswered questions.
  • Delete the question by clicking “Delete” in the unanswered page.

II. Trader Portal

Market

  • Click the Market Icon on the sidebar to access this function.
  • List shows all available reports that farmers have posted.
  • Click “Get Detail” to get more information.
  • Click “Contact Seller” to directly send message to farmers.

III. Researcher Portal

Farmer's Profiles

  • Farmer’s Profiles shows all available profiles of farmers.
  • There are three different pages – list page, detail page, favorite page.
  • Click the Farmer’s Profiles Icon on the sidebar to access list page.
  • List Page shows a list of available profiles of farmers.
  • Click “Favorite” button on the detail page to add a specific farmer to researchers’ favorite list.
  • Click the Favorite Icon on the sidebar to access the favorite page.
